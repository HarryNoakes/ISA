Phishing education made easy

Is this a phish is an online training tool that aims to educate on how to prevent yourself from becoming the victim of a phishing attack. The method of this is by providing sample legitimate and illegitimate emails and allowing the user to identify which ones they think are legitimate or illegitimate. The tool also gives educational hints and tests to check your progress.
